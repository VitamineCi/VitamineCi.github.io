Dear professors, I feel so glad to meet all of you here. My name is Zhao Xuyang, 25 years old, and I come from Henan Province.

Next, I will make a self-introduction from the following four aspects: my learning experience, practical experience, and motivation for postgraduate entrance examination.

First, I systematically studied all the courses concerning Computer Science and Technology and achieved great results. I have won the first-class scholarships and the National Encouragement Scholarship multiple times. In addition to my academic performance, I actively participated in subject competitions and won many prizes such as the second prize of a school-level programming competition, the second prize of the Lan Qiao Cup competition, etc. In addition, I have been serving as the class monitor since I was a freshman in college, which equipped me with organizational skills while handling class affairs.

Secondly, I have actively participated in the teacher’s projects since my sophomore year, and completed the development of a company’s portal website and WeChat applet. In these two projects, I played a role as the project leader, responsible for back-end development, data collection and writing. It was this process that I realized the importance of teamwork and equipped me with information retrieval and article writing skills.

Finally, I’d like to talk about why I took the postgraduate entrance examination. Through the study and project experience in the past few years, I have established a strong interest in my major. I hope I can gain more professional knowledge. The Artificial Intelligence Research Institute of Anhui University is an ideal place that I have always dreamed of furthering my study in.

I want to improve my scientific research ability on this broad platform through the years of studying in the future.

That’s all. Thank you for listening. I sincerely hope that I can join your school in the future.
